# Research

This directory is dedicated to online research about our products, competitors, market trends, and AI-related topics conducted with AI assistance.

## Purpose

Store and organize:
- Product research and analysis
- Competitive intelligence
- Market trends and insights
- Technology evaluations
- User feedback analysis
- Industry best practices
- AI tool comparisons

## Structure

Research should be organized by:
- Topic or product area
- Date of research
- Research questions and findings
- Sources and references
- Actionable insights

## Conducting Research with AI

1. Define clear research questions
2. Use AI tools to gather information
3. Document sources and methodologies
4. Summarize key findings
5. Identify actionable insights
6. Update regularly as new information becomes available

## Adding New Research

Use the template provided in `research-template.md` to document your research findings.

## Research Categories

- **Product Research**: Features, usability, performance
- **Competitive Analysis**: Competitor products and strategies
- **Market Research**: Trends, opportunities, customer needs
- **Technical Research**: Technologies, tools, frameworks
- **User Research**: Feedback, behavior, preferences

## Examples

- Product feature comparison analysis
- Market trend reports
- Technology stack evaluation
- User sentiment analysis
- Best practices research
