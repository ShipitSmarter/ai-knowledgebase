## Product: Viya TMS

Viya is a Transport Management System designed for B2B shippers who need to manage complex logistics operations across multiple carriers.

### Core Value Proposition

> "Effortless transport, endless possibilities"

Viya helps companies:
- Reduce shipping costs through carrier optimization
- Automate manual logistics processes
- Gain visibility across all shipments
- Improve customer delivery experience

### Four Core Modules

#### 1. Planning
- Optimize carrier selection
- Rate shopping across multiple carriers
- Route optimization
- Cost estimation and comparison

#### 2. Shipping
- Multi-carrier booking from single interface
- Label generation
- Documentation automation
- Carrier API integrations

#### 3. Tracking
- Real-time shipment visibility
- Unified tracking across all carriers
- Proactive exception alerts
- Customer notification automation

#### 4. Improving
- Analytics and reporting
- Performance dashboards
- Cost analysis
- Carrier performance metrics

### Technical Characteristics

- **Architecture**: Cloud-native SaaS
- **Deployment**: Multi-tenant cloud platform
- **Integration**: API-first, carrier integrations
- **Modern Stack**: Rebuilt from legacy system

## Key Takeaways for AI/Engineering Context

1. **Domain**: Logistics/supply chain - terminology includes carriers, shipments, consignments, tracking events, rate shopping
