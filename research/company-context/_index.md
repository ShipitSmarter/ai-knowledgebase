## Key Insights

1. **Viya's main competitors** span enterprise (Oracle, SAP TM, Blue Yonder, Transporeon), mid-market (Cargoson), visibility platforms (project44), and e-commerce (Sendcloud)
2. **TMS market growing 60%** from $1.3B (2019) to $2.1B (2024) per Gartner
3. **European focus is a differentiator** - Viya's 25+ years experience positions it well against US-centric solutions
4. **AI/ML is becoming table stakes** - competitors prominently feature AI capabilities (Blue Yonder's Cognitive Solutions, SAP Joule)
5. **Network effects matter** - Transporeon (180K carriers) and project44 (240K carriers) have built competitive moats
6. **Enterprise TMS requires ERP lock-in** - SAP TM requires S/4HANA, giving Viya a standalone advantage for mid-market
