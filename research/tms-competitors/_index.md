## Overview

This folder contains competitive analysis of TMS providers relevant to ShipitSmarter/Viya TMS positioning.

### Key Competitor Categories

1. **Enterprise TMS Platforms** - Large-scale platforms serving shippers, carriers, and retailers
2. **Network-Based Solutions** - Platforms with extensive carrier networks
3. **Point Solutions** - Specialized tools (freight audit, yard management, visibility)
4. **Regional Players** - Solutions focused on specific geographic markets
