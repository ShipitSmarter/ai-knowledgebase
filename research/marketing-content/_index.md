## Key Frameworks

### Copywriting Formulas
- **PAS** (Problem-Agitate-Solution) - Most reliable for sales
- **FAB** (Features-Advantages-Benefits) - For product copy
- **AIDA** (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action) - Classic, needs web adaptation
- **4Ps** (Problem-Promise-Proof-Proposal) - For lead gen

### Persuasion Principles
- Cialdini's 6+1: Reciprocity, Scarcity, Authority, Consistency, Liking, Social Proof, Unity
- Loss aversion, anchoring, paradox of choice

## Quick Reference

**Headline that works:** `Who Else Wants {Desirable Outcome}?`

**CTA that works:** Specific benefit + low friction ("Start your free 14-day trial")

**Reading level:** 6th-8th grade for mass market, 8th-10th for B2B

**AI writing rule:** The less it sounds like "AI wrote this," the better you're using AI.
