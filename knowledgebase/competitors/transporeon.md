## Product Portfolio

Organized into 4 hubs:

### 1. Freight Marketplace
- Freight sourcing and tendering
- AI-powered procurement (70% efficiency gain in quoting)
- Spot market access

### 2. Execution & Visibility
- Transport planning and assignment
- Real-time tracking
- Predictive ETAs
- Carrier communication

### 3. Dock & Yard Management
- Time slot scheduling (29M slots/year booked)
- Yard visibility
- eCMR (electronic proof of delivery)

### 4. Freight Audit
- Automated billing verification
- Self-billing
- Cost benchmarking

## Pricing

### Market Position

Transporeon operates in the **enterprise freight management** segment, focused on road freight and industrial shippers. Their carrier network and dock scheduling capabilities are market-leading in Europe. The Trimble acquisition signals intent to compete globally with Oracle TMS, SAP TM, and project44.
