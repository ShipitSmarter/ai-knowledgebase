# nShift

**Category:** Delivery & Experience Management Platform  
**HQ:** London & Oslo (dual headquarters)  
**Founded:** 2021 (merger of Unifaun, Consignor, Transsmart, Returnado, Webshipper)  
**Scale:** 20,000+ customers, 1B+ shipments/year

## Target Market

| Segment | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Primary** | Mid-market to enterprise |
| **Industries** | Retail/e-commerce, manufacturing, wholesale, 3PL, healthcare |
| **Geography** | Nordic/Benelux core, 190 countries reach |

## Pricing

**Model:** Quote-based (no public pricing)

Three tiers with features scaling:
- **Essential** - Startups, business hours support
- **Professional** - Scale-ups, extended support, routing rules
- **Premium** - Enterprise/3PLs, 24x7 support, dangerous goods, consolidation

## Key Differentiators

1. **Carrier network** - 1,000+ carriers (largest library claim)
2. **Full journey** - "Checkout to returns" positioning
3. **Integration depth** - 450+ software integrations (ERP, WMS, e-commerce)
4. **Post-purchase focus** - Branded tracking, WISMO reduction
5. **Sustainability** - Emissions tracking, CSRD compliance support

## Competitive Observations

- **Nordic-first strategy** - Strong home market in Scandinavia/Benelux before global expansion
- **Consolidation play** - Grew through acquisitions rather than organic
- **Experience over shipping** - Positioning beyond "shipping software" to "delivery experience management"
- **Enterprise focus** - Quote-based model, complex integrations suggest larger customers
- **Webshipper funnel** - SMB brand serves as entry point, upgrades to nShift
