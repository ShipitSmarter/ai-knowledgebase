# Competitors

Competitor profiles for the TMS/shipping software market.

## Profiles

| Competitor | Segment | Geography | Key Differentiator |
|------------|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| [nShift](./nshift.md) | Mid-market to Enterprise | Nordic/Benelux, global | 1,000+ carriers, full delivery journey |
| [Sendcloud](./sendcloud.md) | SMB to Mid-market | Europe (NL-based) | Freemium model, 160+ carriers, e-commerce native |
| [e2open](./e2open.md) | Large Enterprise | Global (US-based) | Full supply chain platform, INTTRA ocean network |
| [Transporeon](./transporeon.md) | Enterprise | Europe (DE-based, Trimble) | 180K carriers, AI procurement, dock scheduling |

## Market Segments

- **Enterprise TMS** - Complex freight, ERP integration, custom solutions
- **Mid-market Shipping** - Multi-carrier management, automation
- **SMB E-commerce** - Self-service, transparent pricing, e-commerce platform integrations
