# Strategy 2025-2030

## **1️⃣ Winning Aspiration**

> “To achieve €12 million ARR in Europe while improving the daily work lives of 10,000 European logistics professionals — by delivering an awesome logistics platform.”

**Why it works:**

- Specific & measurable: €12M ARR and 10,000 workers impacted
- Dual focus: business growth + social sustainability
- Awesome: implies an emphasises for user experience
- European focus: targets our geography and market

---

## **2️⃣ Where to Play**

**Customer Segments:**

- Focus on B2B shipping scenarios, grow both revenue and functionality with enterprises, extend with Mid-market when our product experience provides a faster and better onboarding and a good market fit.
- Primary Mode of Transport Road and Express, support for other MOTs through 4PL providers
- Enterprise companies: multi-site logistics operations with a significant application user base (hundreds of users) and a €10M+ transport spend. Expected MRR 25k+.
- Mid-market companies: single- or few site logistics, having 50+ logistics employees and up to €10M transport spend. Expected MRR 7k+.
- Micro- and small companies are avoided, until we feel it is time to search for scale-ups. Expected MRR 7k-.

**Industries / Products:**

- Companies producing or selling **general cargo** with medium to high shipment value to allow for platform costs, preferably looking for an improved customer (receiver) experience.
- Cargo types excluded **food and bulk goods.**
- Primary sectors: manufacturing, spare-parts and life sciences

**Geography:**

- Head quarters or a major distribution centre in Europe, starting with Benelux, to be extended to other European locations when feasible
- Majority of shipping scenarios within European corridors

**Customer Needs / Pain Points:**

- Automating repetitive shipment tasks
- Improving visibility and tracking for customer and receiver
- Enabling optimisation and creativity in exception resolution
- Agile Carrier management
- Cost optimisation
- Solve lack of Internal IT capabilities and resources
- Securing processes and knowledge through our system, not through people
- High employee turnover and personal experience dependency.
- Increasing flux of outgoing, experienced people
- Scarcity of employees, high person dependency
- Low shipment related data quality, making decision making difficult
- High shipment exception impact on perceived shipper product quality - fulfilment journey / customer journey
- Lack of Logistics Network performance and improvement knowledge
