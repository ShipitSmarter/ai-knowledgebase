Use the product-strategy skill to help write a product strategy for:

$ARGUMENTS

Follow the product-strategy skill workflow:
1. Identify the product area and scope
2. Load company strategy context (Playing to Win framework)
3. Facilitate strategic choices through guided questions
4. Apply frameworks (Playing to Win, DHM Model, SVPG)
5. Write a structured strategy document
6. Validate coherence, clarity, and courage of choices
7. Store key decisions in memory

The strategy should cascade from company strategy and include:
- Product vision and north star metric
- Target users and pain points
- Strategic bets with success metrics
- How we win (differentiators)
- What we're NOT doing (explicit trade-offs)
- Dependencies and capabilities needed
