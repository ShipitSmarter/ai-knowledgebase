# Product Strategy Skill

Write product strategy documents for Viya TMS product teams using the Playing to Win framework.

## Usage

Invoke with `/product-strategy` or ask:
- "Help me write a product strategy for Shipping/Consignment"
- "Create a strategy document for the Visibility team"
- "Define strategic choices for Carrier Portal"

## What It Does

1. **Gathers context** from company strategy and existing research
2. **Facilitates strategic choices** through guided questions
3. **Applies frameworks** (Playing to Win, DHM Model, SVPG)
4. **Produces a structured strategy document** with:
   - Product vision and north star metric
   - Link to company strategy
   - Target users and pain points
   - Strategic bets with success metrics
   - "How we win" differentiators
   - Explicit "what we're NOT doing"
   - Dependencies and capabilities needed

## Frameworks Used

- **Playing to Win** (Lafley & Martin): 5 cascading strategic choices
- **DHM Model** (Gibson Biddle): Delight + Hard-to-copy + Margin
- **SVPG** (Marty Cagan): Focus, Insights, Actions, Management

## Output

Creates a strategy document at `docs/strategy/<product-area>-strategy.md`
